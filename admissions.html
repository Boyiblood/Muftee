Admission Requirements

Applicants must meet specific academic and age requirements for undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

Undergraduate Programs

  • Minimum of five O'level credits, including English and Mathematics
  • Competitive UTME score

Postgraduate Programs

  • Minimum of a bachelor's degree with a second-class lower division
  • Submission of transcripts and letters of recommendation
